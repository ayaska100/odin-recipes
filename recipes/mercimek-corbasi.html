Mercimek Çorbası

Description

Delectable little bowl of soup. Easy to make, incredible bang for your effort

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • white onion
  • carrot
  • red (not green!) lentils
  • tomato paste
  • lemon juice (fresh is better!)
  • spices
    • salt
    • black pepper
    • cumin
    • paprika
    • dash of cayenne (optional but recommended!)

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, sautee onions in olive oil until aromatic.
  2. Add tomato paste and carrots, and let cook for several minutes more.
  3. Add your red lentils, water, and spices to the pot, stir well.
  4. Reduce to a simmer and cook for about 20-30 minutes, or until lentils are very soft.
  5. (optional) Blend well with immersion blender!
  6. Most important of all. serve with a sprinkle of lemon juice on top. Without the lemon juice this soup falls flat!
